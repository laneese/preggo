Dear Mama,

Memories are not meant to be rented, trapped behind corporate passwords, or erased when a subscription expires. We built this journal to belong entirely and exclusively to you.

Every craving logged, every fear written in the quiet hours, every bump photo, every name you considered — all of it lives securely within the private, local ecosystem of your own web browser. No trackers. No external servers. No one can see this but you.

To make certain these pages travel with you to your next device, and eventually find their way into your daughter's hands twenty-five years from now — click the button below. It packages everything into a single backup file that is forever yours.

Save it somewhere that matters. Your Google Drive. A USB drive in a box with her hospital bracelet. Emailed to yourself on her first birthday.

This journal is not an app. It will not be discontinued. HTML is the language the entire internet is built on — it worked in 1995 and it will work in 2045. Your story is safe.
Warmly,
The Ever After Memory Co. Team
